Welcome to Footmatch!

Many times, in order to go to the stadium, you are faced with huge queues or applications that are too complicated. Is it really worth it? Is there an alternative method? Well yes. Footmatch is the answer to all this. This new web app was created with the intention of simplifying the purchase of tickets for your favorite teams and beyond!

What is Footmatch?

Footmatch allows you to search for the single ticket by entering the team name, match date, and competition round number. Alternatively, you can search for available tickets for a single team, tickets available for all teams on a single date, or view tickets for a single round of competitions. By default, the web app shows the ten matches closest to today's date.

How to Use Footmatch?

What to do to be able to use Footmatch? Well, it's simple. All you need to do is register and log in with your account to enjoy an innovative and efficient app.

Available Competitions

The competitions available are limited to Italian teams (Serie A, Champions League, Europa League, and Conference League) and, once tickets have been found, they can be added to the cart to be purchased later.
